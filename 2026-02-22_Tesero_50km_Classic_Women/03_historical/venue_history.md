## Val di Fiemme as a Nordic Skiing Venue

The Tesero Cross-Country Skiing Stadium in Val di Fiemme, Trentino, Italy is one of the premier Nordic skiing venues in the world. It has a rich history of hosting major international cross-country skiing competitions.

## Venue Significance for 2026 Olympics

Val di Fiemme was selected as the cross-country skiing venue for the Milano Cortina 2026 Olympic Winter Games due to:
- Its extensive history hosting major international Nordic events
- World-class infrastructure and course homologation
- Reliable snow conditions in the Trentino Dolomites
- Proximity to other Milano Cortina 2026 competition venues
- Strong local organizing experience from four World Championships

The 2025 World Championships at this same venue provide the most recent competitive data for course conditions and athlete performance at Tesero.
