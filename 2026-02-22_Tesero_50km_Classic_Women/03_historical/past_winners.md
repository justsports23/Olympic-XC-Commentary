# Past Winners — Olympic Women's Marathon XC Event
**Context:** Women's 50km Mass Start Classic — Milano Cortina 2026 Olympic Winter Games
**Date:** Sunday, 22 February 2026

---

## IMPORTANT NOTE

**The Milano Cortina 2026 Women's 50km Mass Start Classic is the FIRST TIME women will race 50km at the Olympic Winter Games.** There are no past winners of a women's Olympic 50km event.

The women's marathon event at previous Olympics was contested at **30km**. The distance was equalized with the men's program (50km) starting at these Games, marking a historic moment for gender equality in cross-country skiing.

## Historical Context

- The women's Olympic marathon event alternates between classic and freestyle technique at each Games.
- At Milano Cortina 2026, the technique is **Classic**, consistent with the pattern (2018 was Classic, 2022 was Free, 2026 is Classic).
- The extension to 50km aligns the women's program with the men's, which has featured a 50km marathon event since the 1924 Chamonix Olympics (the first Winter Games).
