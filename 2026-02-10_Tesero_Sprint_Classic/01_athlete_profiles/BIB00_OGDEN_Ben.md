## Personal Life & Commentary Notes

### Family — A Deeply Emotional Story
- **Full name: Benjamin "Ben" Ogden.** Born **February 13, 2000** in **Landgrove, Vermont** (population <200).
- **Father: John Stewart Ogden (1967-2023)** — former XC skier at Middlebury College, coached the **Bill Koch Youth Ski League**. Was a close personal friend of **Bill Koch** himself (1976 Olympic silver medalist) who lived nearby. **John battled rectal cancer for a decade** — watching his son make the Olympics and win World Cup races — before **passing away in June 2023 at age 56.** [VTDigger](https://vtdigger.org/2026/01/04/u-s-nordic-skiings-future-is-a-vermonter-with-a-pioneering-family-past/)
- Ben: *"My entire life has been influenced by the guiding words and actions of my wonderful father."*
- John's final wish: *"Spend a little extra time outside, be it on foot, on a bike, or on skis."* [NENSA](https://nensa.net/2023/06/28/remembering-john-ogden/)
- **Mother: Andrea Ogden.** **Sister: Katharine** (Dartmouth, US Ski Team). **Sister: Charlotte** (Middlebury, father's alma mater).
- Family roots in Vermont since **great-grandfather arrived before the 1929 crash**. Three generations of Ogdens in the Green Mountains.

### The Bill Koch Connection
- Grew up skiing on **Bill Koch's property** — Koch built a ski park for local kids. Ben's father coached the league named after their neighbor and friend.

### Education
- **UVM**: Two-time **NCAA champion**. Graduated with **Mechanical Engineering** degree (2022). "Pipe dream" to work for **Beta Technologies** (electric aviation in VT).
