## Technique & Format Profile
- **Classic strength:** **Elite — the best in the world.** Flawless diagonal stride, explosive herringbone, devastating double-pole. His classic sprint is virtually unbeatable.
- **Freestyle strength:** **Elite.** Equally dominant in skating technique, though his only sprint loss this season came in a freestyle event (Davos QF).
- **Sprint ability:** **The greatest sprinter in cross-country skiing history.** 60+ WC sprint victories. Two-time defending Olympic sprint champion.
- **Distance preference:** All-around — equally capable at sprint and distance up to skiathlon/30-50km
- **Best format:** Sprint heats (knockout format) — his tactical awareness, acceleration, and sustained power are unmatched
- **Climbing ability:** **Exceptional.** On this Olympic course, he opened a 3-second gap on the main climb alone at the January test event.

### Family — "Team Klæbo"
- **Father: Håkon Klæbo** — personal **manager**. Diagnosed with **leukemia when Johannes was 9**; forged deep family resilience. [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/news/cross-country-grandfather-effect-how-82-year-old-coach-kare-hosflot-built-norway-star-johannes-klaebo)
- **Mother: Elisabeth Høsflot Klæbo** — manages **finances** for the family operation.
- **Brother: Ola Høsflot Klæbo** — **media manager** and filmmaker. Also a **musician** performing as **OKEY** (Sony artist, 20,000+ crowd at Bislett). Co-directed the 2025 *Klæbo* documentary. Johannes: *"I have nothing but my brother's music on the playlist."* [Archyworldys](https://www.archyworldys.com/ola-hosflot-klaebo-music-therefore-he-changed-the-name-klaebo/)
- **Sister: Ane Høsflot Klæbo** — lower public profile.
- **Grandmother: Mildrid Høsflot** — bakes **special bread in bulk** for Johannes due to his **food allergy**; he brings it to every competition worldwide. [ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/klaebo-the-superstar-of-modern-cross-country-skiing/)

**COMMENTARY NOTE:** The Klæbo operation is a true family enterprise — grandfather coaches, father manages, mother handles finances, brother films/produces music, grandmother bakes the bread he can't travel without.

### Grandfather & Coach: Kåre Høsflot (age 83)
- Personal head coach **since childhood**. They live 4 miles apart in Trondheim and meet **daily**.
- Gave Johannes his **first pair of skis** (used, re-gifted from a cousin) **for Christmas 1998 at age 2**. Family legend: **he skied around the living room all Christmas Day.** [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/news/cross-country-grandfather-effect-how-82-year-old-coach-kare-hosflot-built-norway-star-johannes-klaebo)
- As a boy, Johannes **cycled 10km each way** just to visit his grandfather.
- At 16, torn between football and skiing: *"I liked the part that if something goes wrong, I can blame myself."* Kåre: *"He was just really down from the first second I said that I want to be the best cross-country skier in the world."*
- *"He's my trainer, he's my coach, he's my grandpa, and he's also my best friend… I've never had to call him 'coach.' It's always been grandpa from the very start."*
- **Present at trackside in Tesero** at age 83.
