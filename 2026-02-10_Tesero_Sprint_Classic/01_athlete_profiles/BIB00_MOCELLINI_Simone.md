## Personal Life & Commentary Notes

### Hometown & Background
- From **Pianello di Tezze di Grigno**, a tiny village in Trentino's Dolomite region — just up the valley from Val di Fiemme where these Olympics are held.
- Parents were **cross-country skiing enthusiasts** who steered him toward the sport. Started at **Sci Club Tezze**, developed at **US Primiero**, then joined **GS Fiamme Gialle**.
- A surprise celebration was organized in his tiny hometown after his 2022 World Cup podium. [FISI Trentino](https://www.fisitrentino.it/notizie.asp?n=157334&l=0)

### The Fiamme Gialle System — For International Viewers
- **Fiamme Gialle** ("Yellow Flames") is the elite sports division of Italy's **Guardia di Finanza** (financial police). Athletes get a military rank and salary while training full-time. The skiing unit is based in **Predazzo** — just up the valley from Tesero. Founded **1925**, produced **80 Olympic medals** (28 gold). [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/GS_Fiamme_Gialle)

### The Canmore Crash — Full Story
- **Feb 8, 2024:** Training crash in Canmore, Canada — *"flew over protective fencing and fell five meters off the track."* Diagnosis: **broken tibia and fibula**. Coach Markus Cramer: *"A very complicated broken leg."* [FasterSkier](https://fasterskier.com/2024/02/olympic-medalists-and-rookies-rub-shoulders-in-canmore-as-world-cup-returns-to-north-america/)
- **April 2025:** Surgery. Through mid-June 2025, still on crutches.
- *"We didn't try to hurry the stages and that's precisely why things went faster in the end."* [Isokinetic](https://isokineticmagazine.com/en/2025/09/simone-mocellini-chooses-isokinetic-verona-to-regain-his-form-an-extraordinary-centre-with-exceptional-people/)
