## Personal Life & Commentary Notes

### Family — Five Generations in Tahoe
- **Full name: James Clinton Schoonmaker.** Born **April 12, 2000** in **Tahoe City, California**.
- **Parents: Dirk and Marty Schoonmaker.** Father played soccer and lacrosse at Swarthmore College. Mother introduced JC to skiing — pulled him in a sled on Tahoe XC trails before he could walk.
- **Five generations in Tahoe:** Great-great-grandfather **Carl Bechdolt Sr.** arrived at Lake Tahoe's North Shore in **1906**. Great-grandfather "The Hawk" was a 1930s skier and longtime owner of the Tahoe City Golf Course. [Tahoe Quarterly](https://tahoequarterly.com/best-of-tahoe-2022/family-ties)

### How He Started
- Mom signed him up for the **Strider Gliders** program. Earliest memories: *"the raffles after each race — I hoped to win gloves or a hat."* First competitive spark: a middle school race — *"The feeling of competing was so fun."* [Moonshine Ink](https://www.moonshineink.com/sports/nordic-king-qa-with-jc-schoonmaker/)
