## Personal Life & Commentary Notes

### Family — A Story of Sacrifice
- Born **December 19, 2002** in **Trichiana di Borgo Valbelluna**, Belluno province. **Grandfather** co-founded the local XC club — multi-generational skiing family.
- **Parents: Beppi and Barbara Barp.** When Elia showed promise, the family **relocated to Falcade** to facilitate his training. Father Beppi now **commutes daily to work** because of this sacrifice. [Corriere delle Alpi](https://www.corrierealpi.it/cronaca/bepi-barp-ci-siamo-trasferiti-a-falcade-per-far-allenare-nostro-figlio-is189ee9)
- **Brother: Dante Barp** (born 2007) — also competitive. Elia: *"It would be nice if in the coming years I could train with him."*
- **Girlfriend: Virginia Cena** — also a skier, present at his Olympic debut.
- *"In Falcade we all know each other, it's a small village. Falcade now means home."*

### Fiamme Gialle
- Enlisted in **Fiamme Gialle** (Italian financial police sports group) in 2020 — Italy's traditional pathway for winter sports athletes.

### Pellegrino's Protege
- **Pellegrino is his idol and mentor.** Watched Pellegrino's 2017 World Championship gold as a teenager — it inspired him.
- *"Federico Pellegrino is the athlete I look up to… like a second coach for me."* Called Pellegrino's "right arm" by Italian media.
- Together they podiumed twice in team sprint this season. [Olympics.com (Italian)](https://www.olympics.com/it/milano-cortina-2026/notizie/elia-barp-milano-cortina-2026-vogliamo-medaglia-sci-fondo-intervista/)

### Cardiac Arrhythmia
- After the 2025 Worlds, experienced a **cardiac arrhythmia** requiring a minor surgical procedure. Returned to competition: *"I can go back to doing what I love without impediments!"*
- **First Italian born after 2000** to reach the WC podium.
