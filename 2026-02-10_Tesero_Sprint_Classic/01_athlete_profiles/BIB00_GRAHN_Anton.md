## Personal Life & Commentary Notes

### Hometown & Background
- From **Jönköping** in southern Sweden — not a traditional ski hotbed. Started at tiny club **IF Hallby SOK**.
- Relocated to **Mora** for high school at the renowned **Mora Gymnasium ski program** (studied economics). Now races for **IFK Mora SK**. [Skidor.com](https://www.skidor.com/idrotter/langdakning/nyheter/2023/2023-10-11-grahn-och-somskars-moderklubbar-far-hejapriset)
- On his roots: *"My skiing life started in Jönköping in Hallby. A very fine club that has fostered many good skiers."*

### Family
- **Father** — mentioned driving home skis when Anton got last-minute World Cup call-up: *"Dad got a little worried because he had just driven home with all the skis."* [Dala-Demokraten](https://www.dalademokraten.se/2023-03-16/efter-framgangsrika-vintern-far-anton-debutera-i-varldscupen-det-ska-bli-jattekul/)

### The Norwegian Rivalry
- Norwegian World Champions mocked Swedish skiers: *"The Swedes need to go home and train."* Grahn's response: *"They probably should show a little respect after getting beaten by me."* [ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/go-home-and-train-grahn-smiles-and-targets-olympic-gold/)

### Awards & Development
- Won **Charlotte Kalla's Prize for Best Junior** (2023) and the **Mora-Nisse Stipendium** (past recipients include Kalla and Halfvarsson). [Skidor.com](https://www.skidor.com/idrotter/langdakning/nyheter/2023/2023-06-30-mora-nisse-stipendiet-2023-till-tove-ericsson-och-anton-grahn)
