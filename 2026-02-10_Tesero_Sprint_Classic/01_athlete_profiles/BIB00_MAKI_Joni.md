### The Bolshunov Incident — On His Birthday
- At the **2021 World Championships relay** in Oberstdorf, Russian Alexander Bolshunov deliberately **broke his pole on Mäki's back** after a racing incident. The attack happened on **January 24 — Mäki's birthday**. Bolshunov was disqualified. The incident became one of the most infamous moments in XC skiing history and made Mäki known worldwide.
