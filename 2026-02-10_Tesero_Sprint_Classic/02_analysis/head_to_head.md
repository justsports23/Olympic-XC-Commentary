## Matchup 2: Klæbo vs. Pellegrino — The Rivalry

**The defining sprint rivalry of the era.** Pellegrino has finished behind Klæbo in the sprint at:
- 2018 Olympics (Silver behind Klæbo's Gold — classic)
- 2022 Olympics (Silver behind Klæbo's Gold — free)
- 2025 World Championships (Silver behind Klæbo's Gold)

**This season direct results:**
- Ruka classic sprint: Klæbo won; Pellegrino DNQ
- Val di Fiemme TdS classic sprint: Klæbo won final; Pellegrino eliminated in SF (~10th)

Pellegrino's classic sprint form this season is significantly weaker than his freestyle sprint. He finished 2nd at Davos and Oberhof in freestyle sprints, but in classic format — the Olympic format — he has been uncompetitive against Klæbo. The emotional narrative (farewell race, home soil, potential 3rd consecutive Olympic sprint silver) is enormous, but the form line suggests a medal requires Pellegrino to significantly exceed his recent classic sprint level.
