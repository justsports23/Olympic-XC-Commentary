# Course Profile — Predazzo Normal Hill & Tesero 10km XC Course

## Ski Jumping — Predazzo Ski Jumping Stadium

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Hill Name** | Predazzo Ski Jumping Stadium (Trampolino Giuseppe Dal Ben) |
| **Hill Size (HS)** | HS106 (Normal Hill) |
| **K-Point** | K95 |
| **Location** | Predazzo, Val di Fiemme, Trentino, Italy |
| **Altitude** | ~1,000m above sea level |
| **Capacity** | ~18,000 spectators |
| **Previous Major Events** | FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2003, 2013 |

### Hill Characteristics
- The Predazzo normal hill is a well-known venue in the Nordic Combined world, having hosted numerous World Cup and championship events
- The hill has undergone renovations for the 2026 Olympics
- Wind conditions can be variable in the Val di Fiemme valley, which may affect jump distances
- The in-run and landing profile are considered technically demanding

## Cross-Country — Fabio Canal Center, Tesero (Lago di Tesero)

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Course Length** | 10km (4 x 2.5km laps) |
| **Location** | Cross-Country and Biathlon Center Fabio Canal, Tesero |
| **Altitude** | ~1,000m above sea level |
| **Technique** | Freestyle |

### Course Characteristics
- The Tesero cross-country stadium is located in the Val di Fiemme valley, approximately 10km from the Predazzo jumping venue
- The course features a mix of climbing, flat, and descending sections characteristic of Italian valley courses
- The stadium at Tesero (Lago di Tesero) has hosted numerous FIS events and was used for the 2003 and 2013 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships
- At approximately 1,000m elevation, altitude is not a significant factor
- The course is generally considered well-suited for strong skiers due to its technical sections and consistent climbs

### Key Tactical Sections
- **Stadium area:** Flat finish area that favors sprinters in close group finishes
- **Main climbs:** The course features sustained climbs on each lap that can separate athletes
- **Descents:** Technical descents require good technique and confidence
- **Transitions between venues:** Athletes travel from Predazzo to Tesero between jumping and cross-country phases
