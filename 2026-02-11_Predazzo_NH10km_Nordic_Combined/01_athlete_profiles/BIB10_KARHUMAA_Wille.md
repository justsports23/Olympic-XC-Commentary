# Wille Karhumaa (FIN)
**Bib:** 10 | **FIS Code:** 182985 | **DOB:** 7 June 2000 | **Age:** 25
**Birthplace:** Oulu, Finland
**Club/Team:** Finnish Ski Association | **National Team Tier:** A-Team (Finland)

## Current Rankings (2025-26)
- **World Cup Overall Standing:** 53rd (21 pts) ([FIS Cup Standings](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/nordic-combined/cup-standings.html))
- **Best Jumper Trophy:** 54th (23 pts) ([FIS Cup Standings](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/nordic-combined/cup-standings.html))
- **Best Skier Trophy:** 48th (47 pts) ([FIS Cup Standings](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/nordic-combined/cup-standings.html))

## Career Highlights
- **Olympic Results:** First Olympics at Milano Cortina 2026
- **Continental Cup:** **First Continental Cup victory** at Klingenthal, Germany — won after the ski jumping round was shortened due to changing wind conditions; placed 3rd in jumping with a 78m jump (122.5 pts), then won the XC race ([FIS — First victory for Karhumaa](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2023-24/coc-first-victory-for-wille-karhumaa))
- **National Team:** Promoted from Finnish youth national team to senior A-team in 2020-21 season alongside Perttu Reponen; head coach Petter Kukkonen cited his WC points as justification ([FIS — Finland nominates athletes](https://www.fis-ski.com/en/nordic-combined/nordic-combined-news-multimedia/news-multimedia/news/2020-21/finland-nominates-six-athletes-to-national-team))

## Personal Life
- **Brother:** Waltteri Karhumaa (younger, age 21) — also competed in Nordic Combined. The Karhumaa brothers carried on the NC tradition at Ounasvaara Hiihtoseura after world champion Hannu Manninen's retirement. Waltteri surprisingly withdrew from the national team in 2024 ([Lapin Kansa](https://www.lapinkansa.fi/waltteri-karhumaa-vetaytyi-maajoukkuetoiminnasta-i/5891969))
- **Childhood sports:** Started skiing at age 6 in Oulu, initially as an **alpine skier** before switching to Nordic Combined seeking versatility. Began NC as a preschooler at Ounasvaara Ski Club in Rovaniemi ([Seura](https://seura.fi/ilmiot/urheilu/wille-karhumaa-ja-andreas-alamommo-tahtaavat-makihypyn-huipulle/))
- **Academic excellence:** Grade average of ~9.60-9.70 on Finland's 4-10 scale (exceptional) as a 9th grader ([Seura](https://seura.fi/ilmiot/urheilu/wille-karhumaa-ja-andreas-alamommo-tahtaavat-makihypyn-huipulle/))
- **Education:** Law student at the University of Lapland in Rovaniemi. Completed Bachelor of Laws, began Master of Laws including an exchange semester at Leibniz University. Dream career: lawyer or entrepreneur ([University of Lapland](https://ulapland.fi/tarina/yhdistetyn-hiihtaja-wille-karhumaa-kun-muutin-takaisin-muistin-taas-kuinka-helppoa-pohjoisessa-on/))
- **Work experience:** Selected as rookie of the year at DNA Call Center in Rovaniemi in 2021 — highest commission per hour as a rookie in office history. Also gained experience at Rovaniemi Court of Appeal ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/wille-karhumaa-09600225b/?_l=en))
- **Hobbies:** Tennis, disc golf (frisbeegolf), "all kinds of other sports" ([Sport Europe](https://sporteurope.org/wille-karhumaa-interview/))
- **Most inspiring athlete:** Petter Northug (Norwegian XC skiing legend) ([Sport Europe](https://sporteurope.org/wille-karhumaa-interview/))
- **Most important Olympic value:** Friendship — "Most things are momentary but true friendships will last forever" ([Sport Europe](https://sporteurope.org/wille-karhumaa-interview/))
- **Life philosophy:** "Life is short, enjoy your coffee"
- **Love for Rovaniemi:** "When I moved back, I remembered again how easy it is in the north" ([University of Lapland](https://ulapland.fi/tarina/yhdistetyn-hiihtaja-wille-karhumaa-kun-muutin-takaisin-muistin-taas-kuinka-helppoa-pohjoisessa-on/))
- Finnish Olympic Committee Ambassador at European Youth Olympic Games ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/wille-karhumaa-09600225b/?_l=en))

## Key Storylines

### Law Student Olympian
Karhumaa balances his athletic career with studying law at the University of Lapland. He's one of the few athletes in the field pursuing a demanding academic career alongside Olympic-level competition. ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/wille-karhumaa-09600225b/?_l=en))

### Finland's Third Man
Finland fields three NC athletes: veteran Ilkka Herola (BIB 30), experienced Eero Hirvonen (BIB 25), and young Karhumaa. He represents the next generation of Finnish Nordic Combined.

### Continental Cup Champion
His first COC victory at Klingenthal showed he can win at the international level. The Olympics represent a major step up. ([FIS — First victory for Karhumaa](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2023-24/coc-first-victory-for-wille-karhumaa))
