## Key Storylines

### Junior World Champion to Three-Time Olympian
Vrhovnik was the **2018 Junior World Champion** in Nordic Combined. He then debuted at the Olympic level that same year in PyeongChang. Now at his third Games at 26, he's the most accomplished Slovenian Nordic Combined athlete of his generation. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vid_Vrhovnik))

### Youth Olympics Gold with Ema Klinec
At the 2016 Winter Youth Olympics, Vrhovnik won team gold alongside **Ema Klinec** — the future 2021 World Champion in women's ski jumping. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vid_Vrhovnik))

### 219-Meter Flyer
His personal best of **219 metres** at Planica demonstrates enormous jumping talent. The normal hill at Predazzo won't allow such distances, but his ski jumping ability gives him an edge. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vid_Vrhovnik))
