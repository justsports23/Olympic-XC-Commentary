### The Kostner Name
The surname "Kostner" is prominent in South Tyrolean sport. Famous Italian figure skater **Carolina Kostner** (2014 Olympic bronze, 2012 World Champion) also comes from South Tyrol, and alpine skiing legend **Isolde Kostner** is from the same region. However, **no direct family connection** between Aaron and Carolina/Isolde has been confirmed — "Kostner" is a common Ladin surname in the area, derived from "Costa." ([Wikipedia — Kostner surname](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kostner))

South Tyrol has produced extraordinary winter sports talent across figure skating (Carolina Kostner), alpine skiing (Isolde Kostner), biathlon, and now Nordic Combined. Aaron Kostner carries this regional sporting tradition.
