## Key Observations

### Germany's Dominance
Germany has won **4 of the last 5** individual NH/10km Olympic golds:
- 2006: Georg Hettich (GER)
- 2014: Eric Frenzel (GER)
- 2018: Eric Frenzel (GER)
- 2022: Vinzenz Geiger (GER)

The only non-German winner in that span was Jason Lamy-Chappuis (FRA) in 2010. This gives defending champion **Vinzenz Geiger** and German teammates Schmid and Rydzek a massive psychological edge.

### Akito Watabe's Remarkable Record
Watabe has medaled in **three consecutive** individual NH/10km events:
- 2014: Silver
- 2018: Silver
- 2022: Bronze (in the LH event)

No other athlete has achieved this level of consistency across multiple Olympic NH events.

### Alessandro Pittin's 2010 Bronze
Italy's only Olympic NC medal came at Vancouver 2010, when Pittin won bronze. He now competes on home soil at Milano Cortina 2026.

### American History
Johnny Spillane's silver at Vancouver 2010 remains the **only American individual medal** in Olympic Nordic Combined history. His success inspired the current generation of American athletes, including Ben Loomis and Niklas Malacinski.
