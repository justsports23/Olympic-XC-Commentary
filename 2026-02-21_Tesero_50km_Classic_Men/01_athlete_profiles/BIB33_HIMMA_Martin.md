## Personal Background

Martin Himma was born on 30 August 1999 and represents the Karupesa Team club in Estonia. He is one of Estonia's premier cross-country skiers and was honored as the national flagbearer at the Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics Opening Ceremony -- a recognition of his status as the country's top winter sports athlete. -- [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/athletes/martin-himma)

Estonia has a proud distance skiing tradition, and Himma carries that legacy as the country's leading male skier. His ability to compete across a wide range of disciplines -- from sprint to 50km -- makes him a versatile asset for the Estonian team.
