## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** Two-time Olympian. Beijing 2022 — 5th team sprint classic (with Graham Ritchie, Canada's best-ever Olympic XC team sprint result). Milano Cortina 2026 — 5th in 4x7.5km relay (Canada's best-ever Olympic relay result). [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/antoine-cyr/)
- **World Championship results:** 5 WCH participations (debut 2021).
  - 2021 Oberstdorf: 27th in 50km classic mass start (3rd-fastest U23 in field of 57), 27th skiathlon [Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/news-item/antoine-cyr-and-russell-kennedy-power-into-the-points-at-world-nordic-ski-championships/)
  - 2023: 4th team sprint (Canada's best-ever WCH team sprint), 5th relay, 36th 50km classic mass start
  - 2025 Trondheim: 9th team sprint classic, 5th relay, 18th 10km classic, 40th skiathlon [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/antoine-cyr/)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** 0 wins/podiums. Best: 2x 4th place — 15km classic mass start at Tour de Ski Val di Fiemme (Jan 2023), sprint classic at Drammen (Mar 2024). [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/antoine-cyr/)
- **Tour de Ski:** 11th overall in 2025-26 (career best). Previously 4th in 15km classic stage and 6th in sprint at Val di Fiemme in 2023 TdS. [Ottawa Sports Pages](https://ottawasportspages.ca/2026/01/17/high-achievers-winter-olympics-cross-country-ski-courses-suit-second-time-olympian-antoine-cyr/)
- **50km experience:** 27th at WCH 2021 Oberstdorf (50km classic MS, time 2:15:31.2), 36th at WCH 2023 (50km classic MS). Has two World Championship 50km races under his belt. [Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/news-item/antoine-cyr-and-russell-kennedy-power-into-the-points-at-world-nordic-ski-championships/)
- **Nor-Am Cup:** 1st overall on Nor-Am Cup circuit 2020 [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/antoine-cyr/)

## Technique & Format Profile
- **Classic strength:** STRONG — self-described as a better classic skier. "There's a lot of striding and that suits me in the classic races" at Val di Fiemme. Career-best WC results predominantly in classic (4th 15km classic MS, 4th sprint classic). Won classic distance race at Canadian Olympic Trials. [Ottawa Sports Pages](https://ottawasportspages.ca/2026/01/17/high-achievers-winter-olympics-cross-country-ski-courses-suit-second-time-olympian-antoine-cyr/)
- **Freestyle strength:** Competent but secondary to classic
- **Sprint ability:** Good — 4th in WC sprint classic (Drammen 2024), 5th team sprint at Olympics
- **Distance preference:** Distance/mid-distance. "Normally performs a little better in distance." [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/antoine-cyr/)
- **Best format:** Classic mass start distance events
- **Climbing ability:** Strong — Val di Fiemme course with its climbs suits his strengths

## Key Storylines
- Canada's cross-country skiing leader — carrying a nation's expectations in the sport's toughest event
- Classic specialist entering a classic race — technique alignment is strong
- TWO previous 50km WCH starts (27th in 2021, 36th in 2023) give him genuine marathon experience
- After his WCH debut 50km, said "Mass starts are chaos here in Europe. It is nothing like we race at Canada and I learned so much" — has grown since then
- Described himself as "a more mature skier, when it comes to race tactics and moving in the peloton" — crucial for 50km mass start
- Course at Tesero suits his strengths — "There's a lot of striding and that suits me in the classic races"
- Career-best Tour de Ski (11th) shows distance endurance is peaking
- Coming off the emotional high of Canada's historic 5th-place relay finish
- Parents were avid cross-country skiers; also works as a bike mechanic at Boreal Bike Shop [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/antoine-cyr/)
