## Key Storylines
- Olympic silver medalist! Riding a wave of confidence from the relay silver won just 6 days ago
- At 25, this is his breakthrough season -- first full World Cup campaign, first Olympics, first Olympic medal
- French team has been a revelation at these Olympics (relay silver was a historic result)
- His 50km WCH experience (21st at Trondheim) is valuable, but that was freestyle not classic
- Learning curve all season -- the 50km classic mass start is the ultimate endurance test, and his inexperience could show
- Described the atmosphere at Trondheim 50km as something "we'll remember for the rest of our lives" -- emotional connection to the distance
