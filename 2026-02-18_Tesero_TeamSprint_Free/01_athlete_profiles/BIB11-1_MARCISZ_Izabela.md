## Personal Background
Izabela Marcisz was born May 18, 2000. Her elder sister Ewelina Marcisz (born 1994) competed at the 2018 PyeongChang Olympics in alpine skiing, making sport a family tradition. Izabela herself chose cross-country skiing. She is based in Sweden for much of her training — an unusual arrangement for a Polish skier — which gives her access to Scandinavian training infrastructure and likely contributed to her technical development.
