## Personal Life & Background
Savickaite was born on 5 November 2004 in Lithuania ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Egl%C4%97_Savickait%C4%97)). She was Lithuania's sole female cross-country skiing entrant at the 2020 Winter Youth Olympics in Lausanne, Switzerland, competing at just 15 years of age in cross-country cross (68th), sprint freestyle qualification (63rd), and 5km classical (65th) ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Egl%C4%97_Savickait%C4%97)). She then represented Lithuania at the Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics alongside teammate Ieva Dainyte, making the two Lithuanian teenagers a close-knit team on the international circuit ([Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/athletes/egle-savickaite)).
