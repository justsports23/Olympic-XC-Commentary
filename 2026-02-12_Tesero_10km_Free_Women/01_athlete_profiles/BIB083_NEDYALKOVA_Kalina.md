## Personal Life & Background
Nedyalkova was born in 2001 and competes for the Alexander Logistics Sports Club based in Sofia, a club that has supported cross-country skiing in Bulgaria for more than 15 years ([Alexander Logistics](https://alog-bg.com/en/news/alexander-logistics-with-25-medals-in-sofia-city-cross-country-skiing-championships/)). She is also active in orienteering, holding a profile with the International Orienteering Federation (IOF) ([IOF Eventor](https://eventor.orienteering.org/Athletes/Details/24245)). A likely family connection exists with Plamen Nedyalkov, who served as event director for international ski orienteering competitions organized by Orienteering Club Alexander Logistics Sofia, suggesting her skiing career may have grown out of a family and club orienteering background ([IOF Event Records](https://eventor.orienteering.org/Athletes/Details/24245)).
