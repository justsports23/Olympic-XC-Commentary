## Personal Life & Background
- **Hometown/Birthplace:** Born on December 28, 2002, in Shawinigan-Sud, Quebec, Canada. Later moved with her mother to the Quebec City region. [Nordiq Canada](https://nordiqcanada.ca/athlete/liliane-gagnon/), [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/liliane-gagnon/)
- **Family:** Her mother is a military member stationed at the Valcartier base near Quebec City. The family's move to the Quebec City area enabled Liliane to develop with the regional club Skibec Nordique before joining the Pierre-Harvey National Training Centre at Mont Sainte-Anne. [La Presse](https://www.lapresse.ca/sports/sports-d-hiver/2024-02-13/ski-de-fond/liliane-gagnon-a-joue-les-heroines.php), [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/liliane-gagnon/)
- **How she started skiing:** Introduced to cross-country skiing at age 6 by her mother and grandparents in the Parc national de la Mauricie, a national park known for its lakes and forests in the Mauricie region. Began competing at age 8. Her first club was "Skinergie de la Mauricie," which no longer exists. Her dream of competing for Canada was sparked by watching Alex Harvey, the legendary Canadian cross-country skier who won World Championship gold in 2017. She became a national junior champion at age 16. [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/liliane-gagnon/), [La Presse](https://www.lapresse.ca/sports/sports-d-hiver/2024-02-13/ski-de-fond/liliane-gagnon-a-joue-les-heroines.php)
- **Education:** [DATA NOT FOUND]
- **Hobbies/Interests:** Enjoys trying to cook new recipes, hiking, and camping. [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/liliane-gagnon/)
- **Quotes:** Her favourite quote is: *"En cas de doute, gaz au boute"* (When in doubt, go all out). [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/liliane-gagnon/)
- **Notable personal stories:** Gagnon represents the strong Quebec cross-country ski tradition and is part of a new generation of Canadian skiers following in the footsteps of Alex Harvey. She participated in the 2020 Winter Youth Olympic Games in Lausanne alongside teammate Jasmine Drolet. [Team Canada](https://olympic.ca/team-canada/liliane-gagnon/)
