## Personal Life & Background
- **Hometown:** Northern Greece — she grew up in a town where winter sports have a presence, unusual for a country better known for sun and sea ([SportNews.bz](https://en.sportnews.bz/artikel/wintersport/biathlon/exotin-in-antholz-aus-griechenlands-sonne-in-die-loipe))
- **Family:** In her youth, she often helped her father in the small family business that specialized in wood processing ([SportNews.bz](https://en.sportnews.bz/artikel/wintersport/biathlon/exotin-in-antholz-aus-griechenlands-sonne-in-die-loipe))
- **How she started skiing:** She tried many sports but left most of them behind after a month. At age 15, she was asked if she wanted to try biathlon at the club in her town. She described it as "love at first sight," saying *"Biathlon is the best, you just practice six or seven sports at once"* ([SportNews.bz](https://en.sportnews.bz/artikel/wintersport/biathlon/exotin-in-antholz-aus-griechenlands-sonne-in-die-loipe))
- **Training base:** She trains primarily in Ramsau am Dachstein, Austria, as part of a tiny Greek biathlon team of just four athletes (one woman and three men). Their schedule is grueling: *"A month on the road, a week at home — that's our rhythm"* ([SportNews.bz](https://en.sportnews.bz/artikel/wintersport/biathlon/exotin-in-antholz-aus-griechenlands-sonne-in-die-loipe))
- **Youth Olympics:** Competed in biathlon at the 2020 Winter Youth Olympics in Lausanne ([Olympedia](https://www.olympedia.org/athletes/138750))
- **Role model:** Italian biathlete Dorothea Wierer — *"I often watched Dorothea Wierer, she is my idol"* ([SportNews.bz](https://en.sportnews.bz/artikel/wintersport/biathlon/exotin-in-antholz-aus-griechenlands-sonne-in-die-loipe))

## Key Storylines
- Charalampidou is primarily a biathlete who also races in FIS cross-country events. She made her Biathlon World Cup debut in the 2024/25 season.
- At Antholz-Anterselva in January 2025, she was the last finisher in the women's sprint but received a rousing ovation from the crowd, with Greek music playing as she crossed the line — embodying the Olympic spirit of participation and perseverance ([SportNews.bz](https://en.sportnews.bz/artikel/wintersport/biathlon/exotin-in-antholz-aus-griechenlands-sonne-in-die-loipe)).
- She started biathlon at age 15 after trying many sports. She trains primarily in Ramsau am Dachstein, Austria, with a small Greek biathlon team of just four athletes.
- Her role model is Italian biathlete Dorothea Wierer.
- She has acknowledged that skiing speed is her main area for improvement — her shooting is relatively more competitive than her trail time.
- Greece, as the birthplace of the Olympic Games, holds symbolic significance at Milano Cortina 2026, and Charalampidou marched in the Opening Ceremony parade led by teammate Nefeli Tita.
