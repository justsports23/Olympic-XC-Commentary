## Personal Life & Background
- **Birthplace & Hometown:** From Engelberg, Switzerland, where she attended the Sportmittelschule Engelberg and completed her Matura (Swiss high school diploma) with top marks in 2017 [leafischer.ch](https://www.leafischer.ch/)
- **Current Residence:** Lives and trains in Davos, Switzerland, the Swiss cross-country skiing hub, since returning from Norway [leafischer.ch](https://www.leafischer.ch/)
- **Education:** After completing her Matura at the Sportmittelschule Engelberg in 2017, she moved to Norway to pursue a Bachelor's degree in movement sciences (Bewegungswissenschaften), combining elite sport with academic studies. She was set to complete the degree in spring 2022 [leafischer.ch](https://www.leafischer.ch/) [leafischer.ch/ueber-mich](https://www.leafischer.ch/index.php/ueber-mich)
- **Family / Brother Fredrik:** An important person in her life is her brother Fredrik Fischer, who is 1.5 years younger and also a professional cross-country skier. The siblings support and help each other in their parallel careers and enjoy spending time together [Fischer Sports — Lea Fischer](https://www.fischersports.com/us_en/Athletes/detail/athleteId/722)
- **Childhood Dream:** Like many children, Lea had many dreams and dream jobs growing up; one of them was to become a professional skier. She is now living that childhood dream [Fischer Sports — Lea Fischer](https://www.fischersports.com/us_en/Athletes/detail/athleteId/722)
- **Hobbies:** Enjoys spending time with her family, her boyfriend, and friends, especially combined with good food [Fischer Sports — Lea Fischer](https://www.fischersports.com/us_en/Athletes/detail/athleteId/722)
- **Career Breakthrough Memory:** She considers one of her biggest breakthrough moments to be skiing the third leg in the relay at the 2017 World Championships in Lahti, where Switzerland won a silver medal while she was still a junior -- a huge moment for a teenager [Fischer Sports — Lea Fischer](https://www.fischersports.com/us_en/Athletes/detail/athleteId/722)
