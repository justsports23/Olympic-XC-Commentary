## Key Storylines
- Hadzic is a two-time Olympian at just 21 years old. She made her Olympic debut at Beijing 2022 as a 17-year-old, and now returns with significantly more international experience.
- She represents Ski Club Sljeme Zagreb, Croatia's primary cross-country skiing club.
- Croatia's cross-country delegation at Milano Cortina 2026 consists of Hadzic, teammate Ema Sobol (also in this race), and Marko Skender ([Croatia Week](https://www.croatiaweek.com/croatia-14-athletes-milan-cortina-2026-winter-olympics/)).
- Croatia had 14 athletes across four sports at the 2026 Winter Olympics — the largest Croatian winter delegation in recent memory.
- Hadzic and Sobol competed together in the team sprint at the 2025 World Championships in Trondheim.
