## Personal Life & Background

### Hometown & Upbringing
Lisa Achleitner was born on January 18, 1997, and resides in Breitenbach am Inn, a small municipality in the Kufstein district of Tyrol, Austria. She grew up in the traditional cross-country skiing heartland of the Austrian Alps, where the sport is deeply embedded in the local culture. ([FIS Athlete Biography](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=cc&competitorid=180662&type=result))

### How She Started Skiing
Achleitner's skiing roots trace back to the Tirolean ski clubs. She joined LLC Region Angerberg-Tirol as a young skier and later became affiliated with Kitzbueheler Ski Club (KSC). Her participation in the 2015 European Youth Olympic Festival (EYOF) as a junior for Austria demonstrates that she was part of the competitive pipeline from an early age. ([LLC Region Angerberg](https://www.llc-angerberg.com/node/802))
