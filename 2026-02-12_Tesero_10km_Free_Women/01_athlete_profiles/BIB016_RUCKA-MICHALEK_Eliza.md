## Personal Life & Background
- **Hometown/Birthplace:** Born on December 20, 2000, in Poland. Based in the Istebna area in the Silesian Beskids mountains of southern Poland, as indicated by her club affiliation with MKS Istebna -- a well-known cross-country skiing centre in Poland. [FIS Profile](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=cc&competitorid=206098&type=result), [pl.Wikipedia](https://pl.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eliza_Rucka)
- **Family:** Married to Mikolaj Michalek (hence the hyphenated surname Rucka-Michalek; she previously competed as Eliza Rucka). The couple has two children: a daughter named Toska and a younger son named Franio. Her husband Mikolaj came to Italy with the children to cheer her on at the Olympics. The Polish Ski Association applied to the Ministry of Sport and Tourism for a special scholarship for both the athlete and her husband/coach. [Polskie Radio](https://polskieradio24.pl/artykul/3644892,dramatyczna-historia-z-finalem-na-zio-eliza-rucka-michalek-czuje-euforie), [Interia Sport](https://sport.interia.pl/zimowe-igrzyska-olimpijskie/polacy/news-to-bohaterka-polskiej-kadry-w-oczach-pojawily-sie-lzy-wspomn,nId,22597798)
- **How she started skiing:** First appeared on the international stage on December 12, 2015, at the Slavic Cup in Strbske Pleso, Slovakia, at just 14 years old. She was regarded as a great talent in cross-country skiing but suffered from vasovagal syndrome -- a condition characterized by sudden drops in blood pressure and slowing of the heart rate, causing her to faint during physical exertion. [Sportowe Fakty](https://sportowefakty.wp.pl/biegi-narciarskie/1221986/cztery-lata-lekarze-zabronili-jej-uprawiac-sport-teraz-polka-wraca-do-zawodow), [Interia Sport](https://sport.interia.pl/biegi-narciarskie/news-dramatyczne-sceny-w-pucharze-swiata-sensacyjny-powrot-polki,nId,22464534)
- **Health struggles and comeback:** In June 2021, due to recurring fainting episodes during competitions, the Polish Ski Association barred her from competing. Doctors could not find a solution. She stepped away from the sport, got married, had two children -- and her health problems disappeared after pregnancy. In 2024, she entered a regional race, then another, quickly obtained medical clearance, and then won the Polish Senior Championships. With the Olympics approaching, she resumed full-time training and qualified for Milano Cortina 2026 after only ten months of training. [Sportowe Fakty](https://sportowefakty.wp.pl/biegi-narciarskie/1221986/cztery-lata-lekarze-zabronili-jej-uprawiac-sport-teraz-polka-wraca-do-zawodow), [Polskie Radio](https://polskieradio24.pl/artykul/3644892,dramatyczna-historia-z-finalem-na-zio-eliza-rucka-michalek-czuje-euforie)

- **Breakout season:** The 2025-26 season represents a massive breakthrough for Rucka-Michalek, stepping up from national/FIS-level dominance to competitive World Cup results.
