## Personal Life & Background
Groetzner was born on 2 October 2003 in San Carlos de Bariloche, Argentina, a Patagonian city that is one of the country's premier destinations for winter sports including cross-country skiing ([Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/athletes/agustina-groetzner)). Her full name is Agustina Groetzner Rocco ([Wikidata](https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q81903263)). Bariloche, nestled in the Andes mountains, is a natural hub for Argentine winter sports from July to October, and Groetzner is one of several Argentine cross-country skiers who have emerged from this region ([Bariloche Tourism](https://barilocheturismo.gob.ar/en/ski-and-snow)). She competed at the 2020 Youth Olympics in Lausanne at age 16 ([Olympedia](https://www.olympedia.org/athletes/139048)). At the 2023 South American Cup held in her hometown of Bariloche, she finished third in both the 5km individual freestyle and the 1.3km sprint classic, behind compatriot Nahiara Diaz Gonzalez ([ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/south-american-cup-2023-first-weekend-recap/)). She is part of Argentina's largest Winter Olympic delegation since Turin 2006, consisting of eight athletes across alpine skiing, cross-country skiing, and luge ([Panam Sports](https://www.panamsports.org/en/news-sport/comite-olimpico-argentino-confirma-delegacion-que-participara-en-milano-cortina-2026/)).
