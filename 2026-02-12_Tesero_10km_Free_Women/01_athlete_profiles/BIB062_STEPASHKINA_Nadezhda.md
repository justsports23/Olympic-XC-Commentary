## Personal Life & Background
Nadezhda Stepashkina was born on September 4, 1998, and represents Kazakhstan in cross-country skiing ([Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/athletes/nadezhda-stepashkina)). She is one of Kazakhstan's most experienced female cross-country skiers, having competed at the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics before returning for a second Games at Milano Cortina 2026 ([Olympics Wiki](https://olympics.fandom.com/wiki/Nadezhda_Stepashkina)). She competes primarily on the domestic and Eastern Europe Cup circuit, based around the Schuchinsk training center in the Akmola region of Kazakhstan, which serves as the country's primary cross-country skiing hub ([Kazakhstan Olympic Team](https://olympic.kz/en/article/35431-olimpiada-2026-skolko-kazakhstantsev-vystupyat-na-igrakh-i-v-kakikh-vidakh-sporta)).
