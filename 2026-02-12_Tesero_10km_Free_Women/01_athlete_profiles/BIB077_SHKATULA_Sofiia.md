## Key Storylines
At just 18 years old, Shkatula is the youngest athlete in this race and one of the youngest cross-country skiers at the entire Olympics. Her emotional story has captured international attention: after competing against Russian and Belarusian athletes in the sprint qualification, she broke down in tears explaining that her parents' house in Ukraine's eastern Sumy region near the Russian border was completely destroyed during the war. "Russia continues to bomb my homeland, my friends, my family. It's still not easy to concentrate on sport here," she said, while emphasizing her anger was not directed at individual athletes. Despite the immense personal and emotional burden of competing under these circumstances, she continues to represent Ukraine on the world stage.
