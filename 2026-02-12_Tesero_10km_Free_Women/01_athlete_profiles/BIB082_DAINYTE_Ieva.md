## Personal Life & Background
Dainyte was born on 24 February 2005 and is a Lithuanian cross-country skier whose spoken language is Lithuanian ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ieva_Dainyt%C4%97)). She was the youngest Lithuanian athlete to compete at the Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics, making her Olympic debut at just 16 years old ([Olympics.com](https://olympics.com/beijing-2022/olympic-games/en/results/cross-country-skiing/athlete-profile-n1041785-ieva-dainyte.htm)). She regularly partners with compatriot Egle Savickaite in team events, and the duo forms the backbone of Lithuania's cross-country skiing program ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ieva_Dainyt%C4%97)).
