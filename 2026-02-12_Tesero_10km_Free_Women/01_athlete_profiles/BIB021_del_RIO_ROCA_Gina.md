## Personal Life & Background

### Hometown & Origins
Gina del Rio Roca was born on January 26, 2004, in **Sant Julia de Loria**, a parish in the far south of Andorra and the lowest settlement in the principality at 908 meters elevation ([FIS Athlete Biography](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=cc&competitorid=239521)). She grew up in the Pyrenees in a country of just 88,000 people where alpine skiing dominates and cross-country skiing has virtually no tradition ([Salomon](https://www.salomon.com/en-us/athlete/gina-del-rio-roca)).

### How She Started Skiing
Del Rio Roca was sponsored by **Salomon from age 11**, an early bet on a young talent from a tiny nation ([Salomon](https://www.salomon.com/en-us/athlete/gina-del-rio-roca)). Growing up and training at altitude in Andorra became a natural advantage, providing sustained aerobic development on courses that reward climbing efficiency ([FIS News](https://www.fis-ski.com/cross-country/news/2025-26/u23-leader-del-rio-targets-tour-de-ski-challenge)). Without a national XC infrastructure, she trains regularly with **Les Bleuettes**, the French women's development team, accessing coaching and competition networks that Andorra cannot provide on its own ([Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/cross-country-skiing-the-goals-of-andorrans-gina-del-rio-and-irineu-esteve-altimiras-1109/)).

### Family & Childhood Memories

I really like the Tour de Ski, especially the Final Climb."* In January 2026, she raced her first Tour de Ski at the venue she watched as a child ([FIS News](https://www.fis-ski.com/cross-country/news/2025-26/u23-leader-del-rio-targets-tour-de-ski-challenge)).
