## Key Observations
- The course was **extended to 1,547m** for the 2024-25 season and retained for the Olympics
- Winning times increased from ~3:06 (shorter course) to ~3:45 (extended course)
- **Svahn** won the 2021 TdS classic sprint at Val di Fiemme — venue-specific winning experience
- **Fähndrich** won the 2025 TdS classic sprint — then finished 2nd in 2026
- **Joensuu** won the 2026 TdS classic sprint — the most recent winner on the Olympic course
