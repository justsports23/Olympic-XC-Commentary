## Olympic Nordic Combined Team Events — Historical Context

This is the first time the Olympics have held a Nordic Combined Team Sprint at Predazzo. Previous Olympic NC team events were held at other venues. For reference:

### Recent Olympic Team Event Results
| Year | Event | Gold | Silver | Bronze |
|------|-------|------|--------|--------|
| 2022 Beijing | Team LH/4x5km | **Norway** (Oftebro, Graabak, Riiber, Schmid) | **Germany** (Riessle, Geiger, Frenzel, Rydzek) | **Japan** (Nagasaka, Watabe, Yamamoto, Nagai) |
| 2018 PyeongChang | Team LH/4x5km | **Germany** (Riessle, Geiger, Rydzek, Frenzel) | Norway | Austria |
| 2014 Sochi | Team LH/4x5km | **Norway** | Germany | Austria |

**Key observation:** Norway, Germany, and Austria have dominated Olympic NC team events for the past three cycles — the same three nations expected to compete for gold in this Team Sprint.
