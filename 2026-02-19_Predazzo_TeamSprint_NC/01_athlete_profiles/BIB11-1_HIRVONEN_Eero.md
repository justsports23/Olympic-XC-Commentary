## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** PyeongChang 2018 — 7th (LH/10km); Beijing 2022 — competed; Milano Cortina 2026 — **BRONZE** (Individual Gundersen NH/10km, time 30:01.9), 5th (LH/10km)
- **World Championship results:** 5 participations; 2 x 4th place finishes
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** 11 podiums — 1 x 1st, 3 x 2nd, 7 x 3rd; win at Lahti 2019 Team Sprint (with Ilkka Herola, by 0.2 seconds over Norway) [Inside the Games](https://www.insidethegames.biz/articles/1075343/finnish-pairing-celebrates-team-sprint-success-at-fis-nordic-combined-world-cup-in-lahti)
- **Other major results:**
  - Finnish National Champion (2022-23 and 2023-24) [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/en/nordic-combined/nordic-combined-news-multimedia/news-multimedia/news/2023-24/finnish-national-titles-for-korhonen-and-hirvonen)
  - Olympic bronze ended Finland's 20-year medal drought in Olympic Nordic Combined [Finland Today](https://finlandtoday.fi/eero-hirvonen-wins-finlands-first-medal-of-milan-cortina-olympics-with-bronze-in-nordic-combined/)

## Technique & Format Profile
- **Ski Jumping strength:** Adequate but not elite — typically finishes mid-pack on jumping
- **Cross-Country strength:** ELITE — known for making up huge ground during XC; rose from 31st to 9th at Trondheim; his Olympic bronze came through dominant cross-country skiing
- **Sprint ability:** Team Sprint World Cup winner with Herola — proven in this exact format
- **Distance preference:** Standard Gundersen (10km) and team sprint
- **Best format:** Team Sprint — WC winner with Herola (the exact pairing racing today)
- **Climbing ability:** Strong — XC specialist who thrives on demanding courses

## Key Storylines
- Olympic bronze medalist — ended Finland's 20-year Olympic NC medal drought
- Quote: "I've always hated this soft snow and I've often suffered in it" — shows awareness of conditions
- Three-time Olympian at age 30
- Paired with Herola — they are a PROVEN Team Sprint duo, having won a World Cup Team Sprint together at Lahti 2019 (0.2-second margin)
- Finnish National Champion in consecutive years
- Cross-country skiing ability is his superpower — can make up 20+ positions on XC leg
- Born in Laukaa (near Jyvaskyla) -- started ski jumping at age 12 at the Jyvaskyla Ski School [FIS Athlete Profile](https://www.fis-ski.com/en/nordic-combined/nordic-combined-news-multimedia/news-multimedia/news/archive/athlete-profiles/article=eero-hirvonen)
