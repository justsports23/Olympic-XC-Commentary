## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** Turin 2006 — 19th (debut at age 17); Vancouver 2010; Sochi 2014 — **Silver** (Individual NH/10km); PyeongChang 2018 — **Silver** (Individual NH/10km); Beijing 2022 — **Bronze** (Individual LH/10km), **Bronze** (Team LH/4x5km); Milano Cortina 2026 — 11th (NH), 19th (LH)
- **World Championship results:** 10 participations; Gold (Team 4x5km, 2009 Liberec); Silver (Individual LH, 2017 Lahti); Bronze (Team Sprint, 2017); Bronze (Individual NH, 2019 Seefeld); Bronze (Individual LH, 2021 Oberstdorf); 2025 Trondheim: 4th (Team NH), 5th (Team LH), 13th (Compact NH), 21st (Gundersen LH)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** 79 podiums — 19 x 1st, 31 x 2nd, 29 x 3rd; 2017-18 Overall World Cup Champion (Crystal Globe)
- **Other major results:** Only second Japanese to win overall World Cup after Kenji Ogiwara (1994-95); Flag-bearer Beijing 2022 Opening Ceremony; 300+ World Cup starts (all-time record)

## Technique & Format Profile
- **Ski Jumping strength:** Elite — consistently strong jumper throughout career
- **Cross-Country strength:** Very strong — capable of winning from mid-pack jumping positions
- **Sprint ability:** Experienced — partnered with Yamamoto for multiple team events
- **Distance preference:** All formats — 10km Gundersen specialist
- **Best format:** Individual Gundersen (19 World Cup wins, 2 Olympic silvers in this format)
- **Climbing ability:** Strong endurance base from long career

## Key Storylines
- **RETIREMENT SEASON:** Announced he will retire at end of 2025-2026 season [Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/nordic-combined-japanese-legend-akito-watabe-to-retire-at-the-end-of-the-season-1023/) — Milano Cortina is his sixth and FINAL Olympics
- Six-time Olympian spanning 20 years (Turin 2006 to Milano Cortina 2026) — Olympic debut as a 17-year-old high school student
- 4 Olympic medals (2 silver, 2 bronze) — the most decorated Asian Nordic Combined athlete in Olympic history
- Wife Yurie Watabe is a Japanese freestyle skier (halfpipe) [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akito_Watabe)
- Brother Yoshito Watabe also competes in Nordic Combined
- Born in Hakuba, Nagano — site of the 1998 Winter Olympics
- Educated at Waseda University; competes for Kitano Construction Corp. Ski Club
- This Team Sprint could be his final competitive event ever — the last Nordic Combined event at the last Olympics featuring the sport
