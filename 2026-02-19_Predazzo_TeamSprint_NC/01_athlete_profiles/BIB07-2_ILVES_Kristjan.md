- **Olympic results:** Sochi 2014 — 41st (NH), 34th (LH); PyeongChang 2018 — 16th (NH); Beijing 2022 — 9th (LH/10km); Milano Cortina 2026 — **6th** (NH/10km, career-best)
- **World Championship results:** 7 participations; 4th in Individual Gundersen LH/10km in 2023 (Planica); 6th in Individual NH (2023); 11th in LH (2025 Trondheim); 20th in Compact NH (2025)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** 12 podiums — 6 x 2nd, 6 x 3rd (no wins); career-best season 5th overall (2021-22)

## Technique & Format Profile
- **Ski Jumping strength:** Elite — won the jumping portion at Milano Cortina 2026 Olympics (132.6 pts, 99m jump), starting XC in first place [ERR News](https://news.err.ee/1609939043/kristjan-ilves-6th-in-winter-olympics-nordic-combined-after-early-lead)
- **Cross-Country strength:** Good but not elite — faded on final lap climb at Olympics, losing lead
- **Sprint ability:** Strong — regularly competitive in mass start and pursuit formats
- **Distance preference:** Standard Gundersen (10km) and team formats
- **Best format:** Individual Gundersen — 12 World Cup podiums, 4th at Worlds
- **Climbing ability:** Moderate — noted for fading on steep final-lap climbs

## Key Storylines
- Four-time Olympian since Sochi 2014 — Estonia's most decorated Nordic Combined athlete ever
- Won the ski jumping portion at the 2026 Olympics but could not hold the lead in the cross-country — finished 6th
- Quote: "The king's throne is open" — referring to battle for Riiber's crown with Riiber absent [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2024-25/ilves-on-how-to-turn-struggle-and-hard-times-into-a-battle-for-riiber-s-crown-the-king-s-throne-is-open-)
- Brother Andreas Ilves (born 2000) also competes in Nordic Combined for Estonia
- Overcame spinal injury in 2019-20 season [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2019-20/kristjan-ilves-sidelined-with-spinal-injury)
- Trained with Team Norway since 2019 — access to elite training environment
- Born in Tartu, Estonia — Red Bull sponsored athlete
- Swix equipment ambassador [Swix](https://swixsport.com/us/article/ambassadors/kristjan-ilves)
