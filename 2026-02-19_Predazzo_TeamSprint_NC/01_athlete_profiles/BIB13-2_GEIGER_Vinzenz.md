## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** PyeongChang 2018 — **Gold** (Team LH/4x5km); Beijing 2022 — **Gold** (Individual NH/10km — dramatic rally from 11th after jumping), **Silver** (Team LH/4x5km); Milano Cortina 2026 — 10th (NH/10km, felt unwell in final km)
- **World Championship results:** 4 participations; 2025 Trondheim: **Gold** (Team LH), **Silver** (Mixed Team NH), **Bronze** (Individual Compact NH), **Bronze** (Individual Gundersen LH); 2023 Planica: Silver (Mixed Team); 2021 Oberstdorf: Silver (Team LH); 2019 Seefeld: Silver (Team LH)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** 67 podiums — 22 x 1st, 23 x 2nd, 22 x 3rd (per start list); 2024-25 Overall World Cup Champion; 7 wins in 2024-25 season; first Seefeld Triple win in career

## Technique & Format Profile
- **Ski Jumping strength:** Strong — consistent elite jumper
- **Cross-Country strength:** ELITE — his Beijing 2022 gold came from 11th after jumping (1:26 deficit), winning the 10km XC in 25:07.7; supreme chase racer
- **Sprint ability:** Strong — team gold medalist, relay format competitor
- **Distance preference:** All formats — Crystal Globe winner across all disciplines
- **Best format:** Individual Gundersen — Olympic champion, WC champion
- **Climbing ability:** Elite — can overcome massive deficits through XC strength
