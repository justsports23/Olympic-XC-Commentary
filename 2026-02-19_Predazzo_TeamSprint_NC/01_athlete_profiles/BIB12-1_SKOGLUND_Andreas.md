## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** Milano Cortina 2026 — 4th (NH/10km) and 4th (LH/10km) — agonizingly close to medals in both events (first Olympic appearance)
- **World Championship results:** 1 participation; 27th in Individual Compact NH/7.5km in 2025 (Trondheim)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** 2 podiums — 2 x 3rd (Otepää, Seefeld — both in 2025-2026 season)
- **Other major results:**
  - Continental Cup victories: Klingenthal Individual Compact (Jan 2025), Schonach Individual Compact (Dec 2024) — dominant Norwegian performances [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/nordic-combined/news/2024-25/coc-andreas-skoglund-wins-individual-compact)
  - Junior World Championships: Gold (Mixed Team, 2021 Lahti); Bronze (Team, 2018); Bronze (Individual, 2019); Silver (Team, 2019) [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andreas_Skoglund)
  - Brother Aleksander Skoglund also competes in Nordic Combined for Norway

## Key Storylines
- Two 4th-place finishes at the Olympics — the cruelest position, just off the podium TWICE
- Breakout season 2025-2026 with first World Cup podiums after dominating Continental Cup
- Partners with Jens Luraas Oftebro (3x Olympic gold medalist) — Norway has the clear gold favorite team
- From Molde — based in Trondheim for training
- Disqualified at Ruka for suit violation — showed equipment compliance challenges early in season
- Quote after Seefeld podium: "It was really fun, it was only my second podium, so I'm really, really happy"
- World Cup debut: March 2019, Holmenkollen (37th place) [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andreas_Skoglund)
- Won Team Sprint gold at 2026 Olympics with anchor-leg sprint finish [Finnish Ski Association](https://hiihtoliitto.fi/en/skoglund-sprinted-nordic-combined-gold-for-norway/)
- 2021 Junior Worlds Mixed Team gold: outsprinted Stefan Rettenegger on anchor leg [Finnish Ski Association](https://hiihtoliitto.fi/en/skoglund-sprinted-nordic-combined-gold-for-norway/)
- Quote: "I was sure I'd be able to beat him [Rettenegger] in the final sprint, so I just had to follow him." (after 2021 Junior Worlds) [Finnish Ski Association](https://hiihtoliitto.fi/en/skoglund-sprinted-nordic-combined-gold-for-norway/)
