## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** PyeongChang 2018 — 40th (LH/10km), 41st (NH/10km), 10th (Team LH/4x5km); Beijing 2022 — 15th (NH/10km), 19th (LH/10km), 6th (Team LH/4x5km); Milano Cortina 2026 — 17th (NH/10km), 27th (LH/10km)
- **World Championship results:** 5 participations; 7th in Mixed Team NH/10km in 2023 (Planica); 8th in Team LH/4x5km (2025); 9th in Mixed Team (2025); 21st in Compact NH/7.5km (2025); 23rd in Gundersen LH (2025)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** None — career best 12th at Val di Fiemme (2021-22 season) [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/ben-loomis)
- **Tour de Ski:** N/A (Nordic Combined)
- **Other major results:**
  - 3x USSA Nordic Combined Athlete of the Year (2016, 2018, 2022) [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/en/nordic-combined/nordic-combined-news-multimedia/news-multimedia/news/2022-23/ben-loomis-named-nordic-combined-athlete-of-the-year)
  - 2016 Youth Olympic Winter Games (Lillehammer): Silver — first U.S. Youth Olympic NC medal [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/ben-loomis)
  - 2018 Junior World Championships: Bronze (10km Individual Gundersen), 4th (5km)
  - 6x U.S. Junior National Champion [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/ben-loomis)
  - U.S. National Champion (compact/large hill) entering 2026 Olympics

## Key Storylines
- Three-time Olympian — first U.S. Nordic Combined athlete to make three Olympic teams
- Remarkable Olympic progression: 40th (2018) → 15th (2022) → 17th (2026) in individual NH events
- U.S. Army World Class Athlete Program member since 2019 [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/ben-loomis)
- From Eau Claire, Wisconsin — started skiing at age 5 at Flying Eagles Ski Club
- Now resides in Park City, Utah; moved there at age 15 for Winter Sports School
- Studying Electronics and Engineering at DeVry University [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/ben-loomis)
- Career-best World Cup result (12th) came at Val di Fiemme — same venue area as this Team Sprint
- Partners with Olympic rookie Malacinski for the USA Team Sprint
