### The "Last Ever" Factor
All athletes know this may be the final Nordic Combined Olympic event. For veterans like Watabe (retiring), Rydzek (likely final Games), and Costa (came out of retirement), this adds emotional weight. The rivalry between Oftebro and Lamparter takes on extra significance — whoever wins the Team Sprint gold claims the title of the last-ever Olympic Nordic Combined champion (alongside their teammate).
