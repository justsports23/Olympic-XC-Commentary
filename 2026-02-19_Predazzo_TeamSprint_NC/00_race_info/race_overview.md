## Format Description
The Team Sprint in Nordic Combined consists of two phases:
1. **Ski Jumping Competition Round:** Each athlete performs one jump on the large hill. Team scores are combined and converted into time gaps using the Gundersen method.
2. **Cross-Country Sprint:** Teams of 2 athletes race 2x7.5km (15km total), alternating legs in a sprint relay format. The team with the best combined jumping score starts first, with other teams starting at their calculated time gaps.
