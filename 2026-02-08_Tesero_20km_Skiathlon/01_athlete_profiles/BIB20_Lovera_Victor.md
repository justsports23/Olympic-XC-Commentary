## Personal Life
- **Hometown:** Born in **Saint-Martin-d'Hères** (Isère), raised in **Le Sappey-en-Chartreuse** — a small mountain village in the Chartreuse massif near Grenoble
- **Childhood:** Started cross-country skiing at age 6 at the Col de Porte near his home. His first competitions were described as "a real ordeal" — nothing predestined him for the sport. At 15, he decided to fully commit [Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info)
- **Education:** Holds a bachelor's degree in **mathematics and computer science** from Université Grenoble Alpes, then enrolled at **Grenoble INP – Ensimag** (a prestigious French grande école for computer science and applied mathematics). Passionate about math and tech — a rare intellectual profile among elite skiers [Grenoble INP](https://www.grenoble-inp.fr)
- **Work experience:** Completed a work-study placement at **Groupe Rossignol** in the Nordic pro service division — combining his love of skiing with his technical education [Rossignol](https://www.rossignol.com)
- **Comeback story:** Suffered a serious back injury in 2021 that forced him off the French national team. Trained independently with Team Vercors Isère, won a French Cup race, reached the European Cup podium, and fought his way back to the World Cup. "I knew I could do it" [Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info)
- **Professional contract:** Signed with the **Douanes** (French Customs sports team), calling it "a real achievement" — cross-country skiing officially became his profession [Douanes](https://www.douane.gouv.fr)
- **Philosophy:** "In the end, despite the ups and downs, I'm still satisfied with my career" and "I measure today the luck that I've had" [Rue des Confines](https://ruedesconfines.com)
