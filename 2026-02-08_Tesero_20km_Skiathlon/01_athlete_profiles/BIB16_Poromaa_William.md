## Personal Life
- **Family:** Father **Larry Poromaa** was an elite Swedish skier who finished 5th in the 1986 Holmenkollen 50km. Mother **Anette Fanqvist** was also an elite-level skier. Skiing runs deep in the family DNA — William grew up surrounded by the sport
- **Hometown:** Originally from **Malmberget**, a mining town in northern Sweden. The family was forced to relocate when the LKAB iron ore mine expansion required the demolition of residential areas. The experience of losing his childhood home has been a formative life event
- **Relationships:** Previously dated fellow Swedish skier **Frida Karlsson**, one of Sweden's most famous athletes. Currently in a relationship with **Cecilie Wasler Fone**
- **Mental health:** Has spoken publicly about struggles with **anxiety**, becoming one of few elite male Nordic skiers to openly discuss mental health challenges. His openness has resonated with younger athletes in Sweden
- **Youngest-ever 50km WCH medalist:** Won bronze in the 50km at the 2023 World Championships in Planica at just 22 — the youngest man ever to medal in the event
- **Club:** Åsarna IK — the same small club in Jämtland that produced teammate Edvin Anger
- **Named Swedish Male Cross-Country Skier of the Year 2025**
