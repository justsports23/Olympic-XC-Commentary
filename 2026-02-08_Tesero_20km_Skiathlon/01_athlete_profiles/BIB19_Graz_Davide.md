## Personal Life
- **Hometown:** Born in **Pieve di Cadore** (province of Belluno), raised in **Sappada** — a tiny mountain village of ~1,300 people at 1,200m elevation in the Dolomites. Sappada has produced an extraordinary number of Olympians relative to its size, including gold medalists **Silvio Fauner** and **Pietro Piller Cottrer** (both XC skiing) and biathlete **Lisa Vittozzi**
- **Childhood:** Started skiing as a young child with the local club **ASD Camosci Sappada**. Described as a prodigy ("un predestinato"), winning races from the youngest categories through the prestigious **Trofeo Topolino**. Has dreamed of top results "since he was six years old" [FondoItalia](https://www.fondoitalia.it)
- **Other sports:** Also practiced **alpine skiing** as a youth, and lists **rock climbing**, **music**, and **films** as hobbies
- **Sporting idols:** Grew up idolizing **Petter Northug** (Norway) and Sappada's own legends Piller Cottrer and Fauner — the latter is now vice-mayor of Sappada
- **Military affiliation:** Joined the **Fiamme Gialle** (Guardia di Finanza) at age 19, swearing his oath at the military barracks in Predazzo in July 2019 [FISI FVG](https://www.fisifvg.org)
- **Pre-Olympic quote:** "We've prepared for four years. Now we'll try to give it our all" [FondoItalia](https://www.fondoitalia.it)
- **Character:** "I'm determined because if you live this life, you can't rest on your laurels. I don't want regrets." [FondoItalia](https://www.fondoitalia.it)
