## Personal Life
- **Family:** Both parents were athletes at **Carleton College** in Northfield, Minnesota. His mother is in the Carleton Athletic Hall of Fame for skiing — Zak grew up in a household where Nordic sport was in the DNA
- **Hometown:** Born and raised in **Bloomington, Minnesota** — a suburb of Minneapolis far from the mountains, making his rise to the Olympics all the more remarkable
- **Late start:** Didn't begin skiing until **7th grade (age 13)** — extraordinarily late for elite XC skiing. Most competitors started before age 6. His rapid development from complete beginner to Olympian in 15 years is one of the most unusual paths in the field
- **Education:** **Economics degree** from Northern Michigan University, where he was a 3× NCAA All-American
- **Wife:** Married to **Julie Ensrud**, a Norwegian cross-country skier and coach — their cross-cultural marriage connects American and Norwegian skiing traditions
- **Lives in Minneapolis** and trains with Team Birkie / Loppet Nordic Racing — helping build the grassroots American skiing scene in the Midwest
