## Personal Life
- **Family:** Mother **Eva Nováková** was one of the best Czech cross-country skiers of the late 1980s/early 1990s. Father **Petr Novák** (deceased 2024) introduced both children to the sport. Sister **Petra Nováková** (born 1993) is also a Czech national team XC skier who competed at the 2014 and 2022 Olympics. After Michal's U23 silver, Petra said: "I'm happy that I'm handing over the baton to my brother. Somehow deep inside I knew he would make it" [Czech-ski.com](https://www.czech-ski.com)
- **Hometown:** Born in **Karlovy Vary**, raised in the tiny Ore Mountains village of **Nové Hamry**. The family had to travel regularly to Pernink, Boží Dar, and even Germany for proper ski training
- **Childhood sports:** Competed in **mountain bike cross-country** racing alongside sister Petra — both became Czech vice-champions in MTB [Karlovarský deník](https://karlovarsky.denik.cz)
- **Education:** Studied electrical engineering at **Czech Technical University (ČVUT) in Prague** for two years but had to drop out to focus on elite sport. Teammates gave him the nickname **"Mr. Computer"** because of his intellect — teammate Petr Knop said: "We call him Mr. Computer because he's incredibly smart and very handy with technical things" [iSport.cz](https://isport.blesk.cz)
- **2024 appendicitis crisis:** After a strong summer preparation, was rushed to hospital by ambulance with acute appendicitis. Required surgery, missed the entire start of the 2025-26 World Cup season. "For me it was a huge blow... that was a shock" [Deník](https://www.denik.cz)
- **Mental coaching:** Works with mental coach **Petr Žídek** as part of his support team
- **Patriot:** Has spoken about his commitment to staying in the Czech Republic: "I'm a patriot. I care about the sport in our country having a certain level" [iROZHLAS](https://www.irozhlas.cz)
