## Personal Life
- **Childhood:** Born in Poole, Dorset — England's south coast. Father Frank worked in the oil industry; the family lived all over the world: Shetland, Alaska, Aberdeenshire, and Norway. Left Dorset when Andrew was 1 year old. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Andrew_Musgrave) [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/news/andrew-musgrave-norway-british-cross-country-skiing)
- **How He Started:** First learned to ski (alpine) in Alaska at age 5. Properly got into cross-country when the family moved to Aberdeenshire, Scotland, at age 11. Honed his craft at the National Lottery-funded Huntly Nordic Ski Club alongside fellow Olympian Andrew Young.
