- **Grandparents:** Both grandparents coached cross-country skiing. The family is essentially ski royalty. [Nordic Insights](https://nordicinsights.news/2025/12/10/f-i-s-grants-neutral-athlete-status-to-saveliy-korostelev-and-dariya-nepryaeva-ahead-of-davos/)
