## Personal Life
- **Hometown:** Born in **Borgo San Dalmazzo** (province of Cuneo, Piedmont) — far from the traditional Trentino heartland of Italian Nordic skiing. Now lives in Val di Fiemme where he trains
- **Family:** Brother **Michele Carollo** (born 2005) chose **biathlon** instead of cross-country skiing and competes for the Italian Junior national biathlon team. Both brothers grew up in the **Sci Club Entracque Alpi Marittime**. Their parents put skis on Martino's feet at age 2-3. His mother was reportedly in tears watching his World Cup debut in Dobbiaco [FondoItalia](https://www.fondoitalia.it)
- **Childhood sports:** Also practiced **mountain bike** racing and tried **biathlon**, but chose cross-country skiing because it "has fewer unknowns" [FondoItalia](https://www.fondoitalia.it)
- **Education:** Currently studying **Sports Science** (Scienze Motorie) at university alongside his professional career
- **Left home at 19:** In summer 2022, relocated alone from Piedmont to Val di Fiemme to train with better infrastructure. "I left home for cross-country skiing, but it's not a sacrifice because it's something I love and believe in" [FondoItalia](https://www.fondoitalia.it)
- **Historic selection:** First cross-country skier from the **Western Alps Committee** to compete at the Olympics since the legendary Stefania Belmondo, and the **first ever** from his home club. The mayor of Borgo San Dalmazzo issued a public statement of support ahead of the Games [Targatocn](https://www.targatocn.it)
- **Pre-Olympic quote:** "The emotions are only positive. I have the right energy" [FondoItalia](https://www.fondoitalia.it)
