## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** Two Olympic sprint silver medals (2018 PyeongChang, 2022 Beijing — both behind Klæbo) [Inside the Games](https://www.insidethegames.biz). This is his 4th and final Olympics.
- **World Championship results:** 2017 Sprint World Champion (Lahti). 6 World Championship medals total [Inside the Games](https://www.insidethegames.biz). 2025 Trondheim: sprint silver. 2025 WCH skiathlon: 4th (missed bronze by 20cm) [Nordic Numbers](https://www.nordicnumbers.com) [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com).
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** 21 individual WC victories, 45+ podiums [ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/olympics-2026-complete-guide-to-the-mens-20km-skiathlon/). Two Sprint Crystal Globes (2016, 2021) [Inside the Games](https://www.insidethegames.biz).
- **Tour de Ski:** 4th in 2025–26. 2024–25 Val di Fiemme TdS: 2nd in skiathlon.
- **Other major results:** Italy's flag bearer at the Milano Cortina 2026 opening ceremony [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com). Racing his final Olympics before retiring February 22 [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com).

## Key Storylines
- **The sentimental story of these Olympics.** Flag bearer, four-time Olympian, racing his final Games on home snow in front of Italian fans at Tesero [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com)
- Wife Greta Laurent is a former Olympic XC skier; second child born April 2025 [Olympics](https://www.olympics.com)
- If he medals, it could be one of the greatest Olympic farewell stories in winter sports history
- His 2nd in last year's Val di Fiemme skiathlon and 4th at Worlds prove this isn't just sentiment — he is a legitimate contender
- "Don't look upon Pellegrino as a pure sprinter. He is a master at hanging on." [ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/olympics-2026-complete-guide-to-the-mens-20km-skiathlon/)
- Retiring on February 22 after the 50km

## Personal Life
- **Wife:** Greta Laurent, a former Olympic cross-country skier (retired March 2022). They have been together since 2012, having first dated during their school-age ski careers. Married in Gressoney-Saint-Jean in 2021 (postponed from 2020 due to pandemic). At the church exit, friends and family formed an arch of raised skis. [Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/cross-country-skiing-greta-laurent-and-federico-pellegrino-are-expecting-their-second-child-1031/) [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/news/federico-pellegrino-exclusive-milano-cortina-2026-cross-country-skiing/)
- **Children:** Son Alexis (born December 2022) and son Fabien (born April 2025) [Nordic Mag](https://www.nordicmag.info/en/cross-country-skiing-federico-pellegrino-and-greta-laurent-are-parents-for-the-second-time-0417/)
- **Hometown:** Born in Nus, Aosta Valley, Italy. Lives in Gressoney-Saint-Jean. The Dolomites — where he'll race his final Games — are just across the Italian Alps from his home valley. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federico_Pellegrino)
- **Cousin:** Xavier Chevrier, a champion mountain runner [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/news/federico-pellegrino-exclusive-milano-cortina-2026-cross-country-skiing/)
- **Flag Bearer:** Carried the Italian flag at the Milano Cortina 2026 opening ceremony — the highest honor for an Italian Olympian [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com)
- **Retirement:** Will retire on February 22, 2026, after the 50km at the Olympic Stadium in Tesero. A 17-year elite career ending on home snow. [ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/pellegrino-has-set-a-retirement-date/)
- **Nickname:** "Chicco" [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/news/federico-pellegrino-exclusive-milano-cortina-2026-cross-country-skiing/)
- **Klaebo Rivalry:** After Pellegrino's WCH sprint silver (behind Klaebo), Klaebo teased him: "Another silver!" — highlighting their warm but competitive relationship across two Olympic sprint finals [ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/klaebo-teases-pellegrino-another-silver/)
