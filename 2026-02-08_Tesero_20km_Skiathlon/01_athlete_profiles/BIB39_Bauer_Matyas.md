# Matyas Bauer (CZE)
**Bib:** 39 | **DOB:** April 12, 2004 | **Age:** 21
**Skis:** Fischer

Son of Czech skiing legend Lukáš Bauer (Olympic silver medalist 2006, 2× Tour de Ski champion). Mother Kateřina Balatková is a former cross-country skier. Grandmother Helena Balatková-Šikolová won an Olympic medal at Sapporo 1972. Three generations of Olympic skiing in one family. [ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/matyas-bauer-my-dad-is-my-biggest-inspiration/) [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luk%C3%A1%C5%A1_Bauer)

Grew up in Boží Dar, Czech Republic. Was involved in both skiing and football as a child but chose the sport his parents excelled in. His father says: "If I was crazy about training, I was a weak version of how Matyáš approaches skiing. He sacrifices everything for the sport." [ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/matyas-bauer-my-dad-is-my-biggest-inspiration/)

WC debut Ruka, December 2024 (44th in 10km classic). At the 2025 WCH in Trondheim, finished 30th in 10km classic — his father Lukáš finished 47th in the same discipline at the same venue in 1997. "My dad is my biggest inspiration." [ProXCskiing](https://www.proxcskiing.com/traditional-skiing/matyas-bauer-my-dad-is-my-biggest-inspiration/)

Olympic debut.
