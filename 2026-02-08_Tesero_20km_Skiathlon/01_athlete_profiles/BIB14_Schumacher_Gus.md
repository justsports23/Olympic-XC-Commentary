## Career Highlights
- **Olympic results:** 2022 Beijing Olympian [Olympics](https://www.olympics.com)
- **World Championship results:** 2025 WCH Trondheim: 9th in skiathlon [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.org)
- **World Cup wins/podiums:** First American male WC distance win since Bill Koch (1983): 10km freestyle, Minneapolis, Feb 2024 [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gus_Schumacher). Youngest American ever to win a WC race. First American man on WC mass start podium (2nd, 20km freestyle, Feb 2025) [NBC Olympics](https://www.nbcolympics.com).
- **Tour de Ski:** 7th in 2025–26 — best-ever American finish
- **Other major results:** 2020 Junior WCH sprint classic gold — first American to win individual JWC gold [Olympics](https://www.olympics.com). Born in Anchorage, Alaska. Education: University of Alaska Anchorage (engineering, 2024) [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.org).

## Personal Life
- **Parents:** Born in Madison, Wisconsin. Parents are recreational skiers who moved to Anchorage, Alaska, when Gus was young — originally "a two-year thing" for his dad's work. His father decided they were staying permanently. Family supported rather than pushed him. [Alaska Public](https://alaskapublic.org/news/anchorage/2026-01-29/to-be-the-best-olympian-gus-schumacher-had-to-learn-to-lose) [Gus Schumacher](https://www.gusschumacher.com/about)
- **Education:** Graduated near the top of his class at Service High School (2018). Civil engineering degree from University of Alaska Anchorage (May 2024). Works part-time for engineering firm HDR — enjoys "working my brain doing some more academic-type stuff." [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/gus-schumacher) [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gus_Schumacher_(skier))
- **How He Started:** Learned to ski "as soon as he could walk, using strap-on skis in his backyard." Joined Alaska Winter Stars ski club at age 9 or 10. Coached by Jan Buron (Polish-born) for nearly 20 years — Buron's club has sent half a dozen skiers to the Olympics. [FasterSkier](https://fasterskier.com/fsarticle/the-phenom-next-door-gus-schumacher-looks-to-lahti)
- **Languages:** Speaks English and German [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=cc&competitorid=207090)
- **Off-Snow Interests:** Backcountry skiing, motocross, paddle boarding, video games. Fishes commercially for salmon in Alaska summers. [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/gus-schumacher)
- **Volunteering:** Volunteers for Special Olympics, inspired by "an amazing special ed teacher" at his high school [Team USA](https://www.teamusa.com/profiles/gus-schumacher)
- **Climate Advocacy:** Member of the Protect Our Winters Athlete Alliance; lobbied Congress in 2022 on climate legislation [Protect Our Winters](https://protectourwinters.org/alliance/gus-schumacher/)
- **Relationship:** Single [FIS](https://www.fis-ski.com/DB/general/athlete-biography.html?sectorcode=cc&competitorid=207090)
