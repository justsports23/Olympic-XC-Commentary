# Paul Constantin Pepene (ROU)
**Bib:** 49 | **DOB:** May 21, 1988 | **Age:** 37
**Skis:** Fischer | **Club:** CS Dinamo București

**The oldest athlete in the entire field.** Racing his fifth Olympics (Vancouver 2010, Sochi 2014, PyeongChang 2018, Beijing 2022, Milano Cortina 2026). U23 World Championship gold (30km pursuit, 2010). 2× roller ski World Championship silver medalist. Romania's flag bearer at Beijing 2022 opening ceremony. A true veteran and flag-carrier for Romanian Nordic skiing.

## Personal Life
- **Hometown:** Born in Brașov, Romania — a Transylvanian city at the foot of the Carpathian Mountains [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_Constantin_Pepene)
- **How He Started:** Began with biathlon at age 10 before switching to cross-country skiing [Olympics Fandom](https://olympics.fandom.com/wiki/Paul_Constantin_Pepene)
- **Five Olympics:** Vancouver 2010, Sochi 2014, PyeongChang 2018, Beijing 2022, Milano Cortina 2026. Flag bearer at both Beijing 2022 and Milano Cortina 2026 opening ceremonies. [Romfilatelia](https://romfilatelia.ro/en/team-romania-at-the-milano-cortina-2026-olympic-games/)
- **Roller Skiing:** 2x silver medalist at the Roller Ski World Championships — a summer discipline that keeps ski-starved athletes race-sharp [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_Constantin_Pepene)
- **The oldest athlete competing at 37.** Racing alongside Gabriel Cojocaru (Bib 59, born 2003) — 15 years his junior. The old guard and the next generation, shoulder to shoulder on the Romanian team.
