## Technique & Format Profile
- **Classic strength:** Elite — has improved substantially from earlier career; won Goms 20km classic mass start
- **Freestyle strength:** Supreme — originally a skating/sprint specialist
- **Sprint ability:** Greatest sprinter in XC skiing history
- **Distance preference:** All-around — dominant at every distance from sprint to 20km
- **Best format:** Mass start (tactical master who reads gaps, conserves energy, accelerates decisively)
- **Climbing ability:** Strong but not his absolute strength — 12th on Alpe Cermis final climb; the Olympic course loop is not the same as Alpe Cermis

## Personal Life
- **Parents:** Father Haakon Klæbo (also his manager); mother Elisabeth Høsflot Klæbo (manages finances). The entire family is involved in his career — skiing is a family operation. [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/news/cross-country-grandfather-effect-how-82-year-old-coach-kare-hosflot-built-norway-star-johannes-klaebo) [Life in Norway](https://www.lifeinnorway.net/johannes-hosflot-klaebo/)
- **Siblings:** Sister Ane Klæbo; brother Ola Høsflot Klæbo, who edits and produces Johannes's popular YouTube channel and co-directed a 2025 documentary about him [IMDB](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt38651983/)
- **Grandfather/Coach:** Kåre Høsflot, age 82, has been his coach since childhood. Sets training regimes, analyzes data, and travels with him. They share fishing trips and long runs in their downtime. "Without him, his grandson would not have reached the summit." [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/news/cross-country-grandfather-effect-how-82-year-old-coach-kare-hosflot-built-norway-star-johannes-klaebo)
- **Partner:** Engaged to Pernille Døsvik (2025) [All Things Nordic](https://all-things-nordic.com/2023/01/17/johannes-hosflot-klaebo/)
- **Childhood:** Born in Oslo, moved to Trondheim at age 5. Received his first pair of skis from his grandfather at age 2. Cycled 10km to and from his grandfather's home as a child. Played football competitively as a youth — gave up his dream of becoming a footballer at age 16 to commit to skiing. [Olympics.com](https://www.olympics.com/en/milano-cortina-2026/news/cross-country-grandfather-effect-how-82-year-old-coach-kare-hosflot-built-norway-star-johannes-klaebo) [Life in Norway](https://www.lifeinnorway.net/johannes-hosflot-klaebo/)
- **YouTube:** Runs a weekly vlog channel with brother Ola showing everyday life as an athlete — wanted people to see "what cross-country skiers do outside competitions"
- **Documentary:** A 2025 film (*Klæbo*) follows the family behind the champion, with music composed by brother Ola [IMDB](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt38651983/)
- **2025 WCH in Trondheim:** Won all 6 gold medals at the World Championships held 5km from where he grew up. Crowned in front of family and home crowd — wore a literal crown and his medals for the celebration. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Johannes_H%C3%B8sflot_Kl%C3%A6bo)
