## Personal Life
- **Named After a Legend:** Named after Mika Myllyla, the Finnish cross-country skiing legend who won Olympic gold in Nagano 1998 and died tragically in 2011 at age 41 [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mika_Vermeulen)
- **Brother Moran:** A professional road cyclist. The Vermeulen brothers represent an extraordinary athletic family across two endurance sports. [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mika_Vermeulen) [ProCyclingStats](https://www.procyclingstats.com/rider/mika-vermeulen)
- **Multi-Sport Background:** Competed in cycling for UCI Continental team WSA-Pushbikers in 2018. Also competed in Nordic Combined — won Junior World Championship gold (team) and silver (individual) in 2017.
