# Jakob Elias Moch (GER)
**Bib:** 43 | **DOB:** February 21, 2006 | **Age:** 19
**Skis:** Fischer

Younger brother of Friedrich Moch (Bib 18). 2× Youth Olympic Games gold medalist (Gangwon 2024): 7.5km classic and mixed relay. Carried German flag at YOG closing ceremony. Bronze at 2025 FIS Junior Worlds. Currently a freshman at University of Colorado — the first CU Winter Olympian representing Germany. WC debut December 2025: finished 21st in Davos 10km freestyle (youngest in top 100 by two years). **At 19 years old, the youngest athlete in the field.** A future star in the making.
